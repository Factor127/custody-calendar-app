## Tone & Style Notes

- **Always:** witty, warm, confident, a little flirty, unapologetically grown-up
- **Never:** pitying, clinical, sad, condescending, surveillance-y
- **The vibe is:** "We get it. Life's complicated. But you're still hot and fun and deserve to go out."
- **CTA energy:** invitational, not desperate — "Get her on Spontany" not "Sign up now!!!"

## Brand Visual Guidelines

- **Color palette:** Dark navy (#0D0B2E) background, deep purple (#1A1445) cards, orange/coral (#E8734A) accent borders, purple (#7B68EE) highlights and text accents, white text
- **Style:** Dark mode, modern, clean — the app lives in a sleek nighttime aesthetic
- **Logo:** Spontany wordmark with abstract bird/figure icon (white on dark)
- **Tagline:** "Finds your moments before they slip away."
- **App UI feel:** Calendar-centric, friend avatars on days, orange borders = "you're both free"
- **Creative direction for ads:** Mirror the app's dark UI feel. Ads should feel like a natural extension of the product — not generic stock-photo energy. Bold typography on dark backgrounds, coral/orange pops for emphasis.
