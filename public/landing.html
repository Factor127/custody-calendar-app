What Spontany does

Built for the way you actually plan.

👀

See months ahead.
Plan without asking.

Your custody schedule and theirs — overlaid in one view. See when you're both free tonight, next week, or in July. No awkward questions. Just open the app and plan.

👥

One screen.
Everyone's free days.

6 friends. 6 custody schedules. Finding one night used to be impossible. Spontany shows the group overlap instantly — so you stop polling and start planning.

See who's free. Invite.

Paste an event URL — Spontany pulls the date, time, and location automatically. It shows which friends are available. One tap to invite. Seconds, not a 12-message thread.

Coordinate without
the conversation.

Pickup changes. Schedule swaps. Weekend updates. All the logistics that currently live in a text thread you'd rather not open. It's all in the app now.

Share the schedule with your kids so everyone knows who's where and when. Less confusion, fewer "wait, am I at mum's or dad's?" moments.
