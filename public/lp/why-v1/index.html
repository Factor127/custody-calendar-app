A note from the founder

Why we built Spontany.

Short version: I got divorced, spent years staring at calendars, and realized the hardest part wasn't custody — it was coordinating a life around it.

So I built the thing I wished existed.

Who it's for

Divorced parents with kids aged 0–14.

You've got a custody schedule, and half your mental energy goes to texting "do you have the kids that weekend?"

Spontany does that math once, quietly, and lets you both see the overlap.

Why it's free

Because the best things in life are free! …nah, just kidding.

Simplifying life for divorcees like me is my core mission, so custody sync will stay free. Always.

I'll try to make some wow features you might want to use and pay for later, but that's on me.

So I hope you enjoy this thing I made ;)

How your privacy works

  • Your schedule is visible only to people you explicitly invite.
  • You can revoke access any time — it's one tap.
  • We don't share, sell, or look at your data to show you ads.
  • If you delete your account, we delete your data. For real.

What it won't do

Spontany won't fix your coparent relationship, won't solve your custody dispute, won't replace therapy.

It's a calendar overlap tool. A really good one — and one that's built for the shape your life actually has.

Want to try it?

We're in gated beta. Drop your email — we'll text you when your spot opens.

— Ran, founder
